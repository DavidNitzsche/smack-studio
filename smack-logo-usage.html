Rules
Keep it clean
There aren't many — but these matter.
Do
  • Use the mark alone when context is already established (inside the app)
  • Use the wordmark when introducing Smack to someone new
  • Use the full lockup for pitch materials and formal documents
  • White on dark backgrounds, salmon on light backgrounds
  • Scale the mark down to texture — the tentacles can be indistinct at small sizes
  • Use salmon (#e07868) as the single accent color on the period and mark
Don't
  • Don't force the lockup where only the mark or wordmark is needed
  • Don't use the wordmark at small sizes — it becomes unreadable below 16px height
  • Don't use Funtastic anywhere except the wordmark
  • Don't put the mark on a salmon background — no contrast
  • Don't mix the mark and wordmark in different colors in the same lockup
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or recolor outside the approved palette
