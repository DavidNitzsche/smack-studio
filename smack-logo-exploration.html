Jellyfish Marks

JF-1 through JF-5

JF-1

Round Dome, Minimal

Half-circle dome with flared skirt and 5 straight tentacles. No face, no decoration — pure geometric confidence. Reads clean at any size.

JF-2

Soft Dome + Long Wavy Tentacles

Closest to the reference energy — tall proportions, 7 S-curve tentacles of varying length. Subtle highlight arc inside the dome. Organic, studio-grade.

JF-3

Pudgy / Chunky

Fat wide dome, 3 thick short tentacles. Stamp-like weight. Bold and ownable — not cute, just assertive. Reads as a blob of confidence.

JF-4

Line Art — Outline Only

Same form as JF-2 but all stroke, zero fill. Reverses beautifully on dark. Editorial and premium — feels like an icon system from a design consultancy.

JF-5

Stacked / Compact — UFO Shape

Extra-wide dome, very short fringe tentacles. White highlight arc creates a dimensional, almost 3D feel. Square bounding box — versatile for app icons and favicons.
