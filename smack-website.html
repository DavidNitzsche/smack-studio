Hold & challenge system

Industry-standard hold logic, built in.

When you're on 1st hold and another company wants to book your contractor, you get notified immediately with a deadline to commit or release. When you're on 2nd hold, you can challenge the 1st hold. The industry standard — finally automated.

  • Incoming challenges: Book Now or Release Hold
  • Outgoing challenges: upgrade your 2nd hold
  • 24-hour deadline enforced automatically
