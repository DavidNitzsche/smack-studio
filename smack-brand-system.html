04 — Studio Theming Decision
Do studios customize their color?
This section answers the question. The recommendation is clear. The mockup shows why.
Not Recommended
Full Custom Color Input
Too fragile for Phase 1
Primary Color
#e07868
Background Color
#fafaf9
Text Color
#111827
Problems with this approach:
  • No guarantee of accessible contrast ratios
  • Can conflict with semantic status colors
  • Increases support burden ("my colors look wrong")
  • Requires testing every possible combination
  • Not worth the engineering cost in Phase 1
Colors could break the UI. Not worth Phase 1 complexity.
Recommended — Phase 2 Add-On

Preset Accent Themes

Studios pick one of 6 curated accents. The Smack structural UI stays identical — typography, spacing, layout, all status colors. Only the accent color (buttons, badges, active highlights) changes. Safe, beautiful, on-brand.
