Future add-ons
Add-on
Client Review App
White-labeled client-facing review and approval tool. Studios share cuts, clients mark up and approve — all inside Smack. Makes the platform stickier and closes a real workflow gap. Could be a higher-tier unlock or standalone add-on.
Timing: Phase 3+
Add-on
Mobile (Native)
Native iPhone app for approvals, project status, and notifications on the go. Launch as PWA first — build native only when studios request it. App Store presence could drive discovery once product is mature.
Timing: Post-PMF
Add-on · Premium
Contractor Payments
Studios pay contractors directly from Smack — no more exporting to payroll tools or sending manual transfers. Invoice approved → payment triggered. Could be a flat fee per transfer or a small percentage. High-value premium service.
Timing: Phase 3+
Add-on · Onboarding
CSV Import
Templates for studios to bulk-upload existing projects, contractor databases, and client lists via CSV. Removes the blank-slate problem on signup. Smack provides the templates; studios fill them in and import. Dramatically shortens time-to-value.
Timing: Phase 2
